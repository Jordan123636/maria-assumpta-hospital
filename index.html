About Us

A Heritage of Healing

The Maria Assumpta Catholic Hospital is a faith-based healthcare institution located in Ado-Ekiti, Ekiti State, Nigeria. We operate as a registered Secondary Health Care Centre, licensed by the Nigerian Ministry of Health, serving patients who require care beyond what primary health centres can provide.

We offer a wide range of medical, surgical, and clinical specialties supported by an on-site laboratory, pharmacy, and imaging centre — all operating around the clock, every day of the year.

Licensing & Registration

Licensed by the Nigerian Ministry of Health

The Maria Assumpta Catholic Hospital is officially registered and licensed as a Secondary Health Care Centre under the Nigerian Ministry of Health. Secondary Healthcare Centres are managed by State Governments and handle cases requiring specialist care beyond what Primary Health Care centres can provide, including general hospitals and comprehensive health centres.
